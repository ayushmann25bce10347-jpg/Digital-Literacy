PART B
The Do's

Make the most of your presence.

Optimize your identity by keeping your "About Me" and "Bio" sections updated with what you are studying and what you are interested in. This will help people find you easily.

Use a good profile picture that looks professional.

Be careful with your privacy.

Check your privacy settings on Instagram and Facebook regularly. This way you can control who sees what you post.

Build a network of people.

Follow people who're important in your field and organizations that are related to what you study. Share what you have achieved and what you are working on. This will show people what you can do.

Always check your facts before sharing something.

Do not share something just because you saw it online. Make sure it is true. This will help stop information from spreading.

The Don'ts

Do not post when you are angry.

Never say something about your teachers, friends or college online. If you are feeling angry wait for a day before posting anything.

Keep your information safe.

Do not share your hostel room number, phone number or college ID card online. Someone might use this information to harm you.

Choose an username.

Do not use a username that might make people think you are not serious. Use an professional name.

Do not fight with people online.

If someone is trying to annoy you just ignore them. Fighting online will not help you. Might make you look bad.

Do not bully anyone online.

Never join in when people are being mean to someone online. You should try to make the internet a safe place, for everyone. As a Digital Ambassador it is your job to make sure everyone feels safe online.
